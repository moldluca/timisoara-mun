The Role of Our Advisors

The Advisor holds a guiding and supervisory role within TimișoaraMUN, offering strategic support, institutional memory, and mentorship to the organizing team. With a deep understanding of MUN principles and event management, the Advisor ensures coherence between vision and execution, while also upholding the academic and ethical standards of the conference.

Acting as both a consultant and a safeguard, the Advisor provides insight during key decision-making processes and helps the team navigate challenges with professionalism and clarity.

Their experience contributes to the long-term development and sustainability of the project, empowering the team to grow while preserving the identity and mission of TimișoaraMUN.

Through continuous encouragement, constructive feedback, and a commitment to educational excellence, the Advisor plays an essential part in cultivating a resilient and forward-thinking leadership team.
