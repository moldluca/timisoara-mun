Our Hosts

From the creative energy of Zbor Hub Timișoara to the state-of-the-art classrooms of the British International School of Timișoara, our venues offer inspiring spaces for every moment of the conference.

Zbor Hub Timișoara

Hosted inside a vibrant co-working and cultural hub, Zbor Hub Timișoara brings together entrepreneurs, artists, and changemakers in a space purpose-built for collaboration.

Its industrial-chic halls, flexible meeting rooms, and community-driven atmosphere create the perfect backdrop for workshops, networking, and creative problem-solving during the conference.

Zbor Hub Timișoara
Strada Vasile Alecsandri 1, Timișoara

British International School of Timișoara

Set in a contemporary campus bathed in natural light, the British International School of Timișoara offers flexible learning spaces, high-tech classrooms, and welcoming common areas that reflect the spirit of our conference.

Delegates will enjoy an environment that combines academic excellence with a vibrant international community dedicated to innovation and leadership.

Strada Aurora 8
Timișoara 300254, Romania
