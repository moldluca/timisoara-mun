FAQ – Timișoara Model United Nations 2025

Frequently Asked Questions – Inaugural Edition

What is Timișoara MUN?

Timișoara Model United Nations is an academic simulation of the United Nations organized in Timișoara by a highly trained team of young people passionate about education, diplomacy, and civic engagement. The event aims to provide a realistic, formative, and inclusive experience for high school and university students.

When and where will the conference take place?

The conference will be held in Timișoara from August 18 to 20, 2025, across several representative venues in the city. Exact locations will be communicated to the selected participants..

Is accommodation and transport provided??

No. Participants must arrange their own accommodation and transport. Organizers will provide logistical information and recommendations after confirmation.

Are meals provided?

Yes. All selected participants will receive meals provided by the organizers during the conference.

What roles are available at the conference?

  • Delegate – representing the position of a state within a UN committee.
  • Chairperson – moderates debates, guides delegates, and ensures smooth committee sessions.

Registration and Selection

  • Chairperson applications: available starting June 6, 2025
  • Delegate applications: available starting June 28, 2025

What are the deadlines?

  • Chairpersons: June 30, 2025
  • Delegates: July 25, 2025

How is the selection process conducted?

All applications will be carefully reviewed by the organizing team. ➡️ Only selected candidates will receive an official confirmation email, including the conference agenda.

Fees and Scholarships

Is there a participation fee? Yes. Participation as a delegate requires an administrative fee, which will be announced soon.➡️ Chairpersons do NOT pay any participation fee.

Are scholarships available?

Yes. The General Secretariat will launch a call for 20 scholarships aimed at covering the participation fee for delegates. Scholarships will be awarded based on motivation, academic merit, and/or financial need. Details on the application process will be announced shortly.

When will we receive the full program?

The detailed agenda will be sent to all selected participants after the selection period ends.

Will preparation materials be provided?

Yes. Preparation materials such as procedural guides, committee descriptions, and research recommendations will be made available according to the participant’s role (delegate or chairperson).
