Leading the Conversation on Global Youth Empowerment

In alignment with the EU Youth Strategy 2019-2027, which emphasizes youth engagement, empowerment, and cross-border connections, and the United Nations' Youth2030 Strategy, aimed at guiding the UN system to empower young people to realize their full potential and stand up for their rights, the Youth Empowerment Forum 2025 is designed to equip young changemakers with the knowledge, skills, and networks to drive meaningful impact in their communities and beyond.

Join us in Geneva, the heart of international diplomacy, for an immersive 4-day event featuring interactive workshops, policy dialogues, and high-level panel discussions with global experts. A key highlight is our exclusive study trip to the United Nations Headquarters, where participants will witness diplomacy in action. Additionally, attendees will embark on a guided visit to CERN to explore cutting-edge exhibitions to understand groundbreaking scientific discoveries shaping the future of technology and innovation. The program will also include networking opportunities over traditional Swiss cuisine, offering a taste of the country's rich culinary heritage.

Youth Grant

The Youth Empowerment Forum 2025 offers a seed grant of up to $1000 to support a promising social action project. Application Process

Applying for the Youth Empowerment Forum 2025 is simple and straight forward:

Step. 1

Complete the online application form by providing your personal and professional details, answering questions about your motivation, and sharing any relevant experience (if applicable).

Step. 2

After the application deadline, submissions will be reviewed by our expert evaluators. To ensure a fair process, applications will be assessed randomly to minimize selection bias. Evaluations will be based on motivation, willingness to learn, and leadership potential.

Step. 3

Successful applicants will receive an official confirmation via email, and the results will also be published on our website and social media.

Need Help? Check Our FAQs

1. Who can apply for YEF 2025?

Young leaders, activists, students, and professionals aged 18-35 from around the world can apply for YEF 2025.

2. What does the fully funded scholarship cover?

The fully funded scholarship covers round-trip flights, accommodation, meals, local transportation, and all conference activities.

3. How can I apply for a fully funded spot?

To apply for a fully funded spot, complete the online application form and select "Fully Funded" as your preferred participation category. Your application will be evaluated based on your motivation, leadership potential, and regional representation.

4. Is prior volunteering or leadership experience required?

While prior experience is valuable, it is not mandatory. We look for candidates with strong motivation, innovative ideas, and potential to create positive change in their communities.

5. What are the participation categories?

There are four participation categories: Fully Funded, Partially Funded, Forum Admission, and Self-Funded. Each category offers different levels of financial support and benefits.
