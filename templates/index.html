About Us

Discover what MUN Timișoara is and how we contribute to the development of young leaders

Who Are We?

MUN Timișoara is an educational conference that simulates the activity of the United Nations, offering students the opportunity to understand international diplomacy and global relations.

Organized by students for students, our conference brings together young people passionate about international affairs, global politics, and diplomacy, creating a favorable environment for constructive debates and networking.

We are committed to shaping the next generation of global leaders by providing participants with the necessary tools to address the complex challenges of today’s world.

Our Committees

Our Mission

MUN Timișoara is dedicated to promoting global understanding and developing leadership skills by facilitating meaningful conversations, offering innovative training programs, and creating an environment that encourages collaboration and critical thinking.

We aim to inspire and equip young people with the tools they need to become visionary leaders and agents of change in their communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where inclusive dialogue and transformative leadership generate fair solutions to global challenges, promoting peace, progress, and prosperity for all.

We aspire to become the most prestigious Model United Nations conference in Romania, recognized for academic excellence and the positive impact on participants.

Our Committees

Explore the committees available at MUN Timișoara and choose the one that interests you

Security Council

Addresses international security issues and proposes solutions for current global conflicts. This committee is suitable for experienced delegates.

General Assembly

Discusses a variety of global issues, from sustainable development to human rights and disarmament. Ideal for beginner and intermediate delegates.

Economic and Social Council

Addresses economic, social, and environmental challenges at the global level and proposes sustainable development policies.

Human Rights Council

Promotes and protects human rights globally, addressing violations and proposing protection mechanisms.

Crisis Committee

Simulates international crisis situations requiring rapid and strategic responses. For advanced delegates.

UN Environment Programme

Addresses environmental challenges, climate change, and the development of policies for a sustainable future.

International Court of Justice

Simulates international legal cases and delivers rulings based on international law. For students interested in international law.

World Health Organization

Addresses global public health challenges and develops strategies to improve health systems worldwide.

UNICEF

Focuses on the rights and well-being of children worldwide, addressing issues such as education, health, and protection.

Disarmament Committee

Discusses issues related to weapons of mass destruction, nuclear disarmament, and global arms control.

Legal Committee

Analyzes legal aspects of international relations and contributes to the development and codification of international law.

UNESCO

Promotes international cooperation in education, science, culture, and communication to build global peace.

FAO

Addresses issues related to food security, sustainable agriculture, and combating global hunger.

De Ce MUN Timișoara?

Descoperă ce face conferința noastră specială și de ce ar trebui să participi

Într-o lume definită de provocări complexe - schimbări climatice, inegalitate, conflicte și transformări tehnologice rapide - dialogul eficient și leadership-ul vizionar sunt mai importante ca niciodată.

MUN Timișoara se află în avangarda acestor eforturi, construind punți între culturi, promovând înțelegerea și împuternicind tinerii să acționeze cu scop și viziune.

Conferința noastră oferă o experiență educațională unică, combinând rigoarea academică cu oportunități de networking și dezvoltare personală într-un cadru multicultural vibrant.
