About Us

Discover what TimișoaraMUN is and how we contribute to the development of young leaders

Who Are We?

TimișoaraMUN is an educational conference that simulates the activity of the United Nations, offering students the opportunity to understand international diplomacy and global relations.

Organized by students for students, our conference brings together young people passionate about international affairs, global politics, and diplomacy, creating a favorable environment for constructive debates and networking.

We are committed to shaping the next generation of global leaders by providing participants with the necessary tools to address the complex challenges of today's world.

Our Mission

TimișoaraMUN is dedicated to promoting global understanding and developing leadership skills by facilitating meaningful conversations, offering innovative training programs, and creating an environment that encourages collaboration and critical thinking.

We aim to inspire and equip young people with the tools they need to become visionary leaders and agents of change in their communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where inclusive dialogue and transformative leadership generate fair solutions to global challenges, promoting peace, progress, and prosperity for all.

We aspire to become the most prestigious Model United Nations conference in Romania, recognized for academic excellence and the positive impact on participants.

Why TimișoaraMUN

In a world defined by complex challenges - climate change, inequality, conflict and rapid technological transformation - effective dialogue and visionary leadership are more important than ever.

TimișoaraMUN is at the forefront of these efforts, building bridges between cultures, promoting understanding and empowering young people to act with purpose and vision.

Our conference offers a unique educational experience, combining academic rigor with networking and personal development opportunities in a vibrant multicultural setting.
