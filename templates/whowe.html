Who Are We?

TimișoaraMUN is more than just a conference — it's an educational and cultural experience built by youth, for youth.

Our Mission Discover Timișoara

Built by Youth, for Youth

Organized for the first time by a dedicated team of high school students, our mission is to empower young voices through diplomacy, debate, and international cooperation.

TimișoaraMUN is designed to provide a realistic simulation of the United Nations, where high school and university students from Romania and abroad come together to discuss global issues, negotiate resolutions, and develop essential skills.

Meet Our Team

Essential Skills Development

Through our conference, participants develop essential skills like public speaking, critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership. These skills are not just valuable for academic success, but for life.

Our delegates learn to think critically about global issues, articulate their thoughts clearly, and work collaboratively to find solutions to complex problems.

Timișoara: The Perfect Host City

The conference takes place in Timișoara, a multicultural and historic city in western Romania. As the European Capital of Culture in 2023, Timișoara is a vibrant hub of creativity and dialogue, making it the perfect place to host an international MUN conference.

This dynamic city offers the ideal backdrop for international diplomacy, with its rich history, diverse culture, and forward-thinking spirit inspiring meaningful dialogue and collaboration.
