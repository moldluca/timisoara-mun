Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2025

TimișoaraMUN is committed to protecting your personal data privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: Name, email address, phone number, educational institution.
  • Registration information: Committee and country preferences, previous MUN experience.
  • Usage information: How you interact with our website and services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Processing your conference registration
  • Communicating important conference information
  • Improving our services
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with:

  • Our event hosting partners
  • Service providers who assist us in organizing the conference
  • Authorities, when required by law

General Data Protection Rules

Last update: May 30, 2025

I understand the processing of personal data will be carried out in compliance with the provisions of Regulation No. 679/2016, adopted by the European Parliament & the Council of the European Union, for the approval of the rules on protection regarding processing of personal data and rules on freedom circulation of the aforementioned type of information..

Terms and Conditions

Last updated: May 30, 2025

These terms and conditions govern the use of the TimișoaraMUN website and participation in our conferences. By using our website or registering for our conferences, you agree to these terms.

1. Conference Registration

By registering for the TimișoaraMUN conference, you:

  • Confirm that the information provided is accurate and complete
  • Agree to comply with the conference rules and procedures
  • Understand that the participation fee is non-refundable after the specified deadline

2. Code of Conduct

All TimișoaraMUN participants must adhere to our code of conduct, which includes:

  • Treating all participants with respect and dignity
  • Following the conference’s procedural rules
  • Avoiding discriminatory or offensive behavior
  • Respecting the decisions of the organizers and committee chairs

3. Intellectual Property

All materials provided during the TimișoaraMUN conference (including study guides, logos, and other materials) are protected by copyright. You are not permitted to reproduce or distribute these materials without the explicit permission of TimișoaraMUN.
